# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in StarEnchants a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct which could reasonably
be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainer at **owen@owen.lol**. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may ban temporarily or
permanently any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate.
