# Crystals (item)

This page covers the **physical crystal item** — applying, merging, and extracting. For what a crystal
*is* (a levelless socket bonus) and how to define one in `content/crystals/`, see
[the crystals concept](../concepts/crystals.mdx) and
[defining a crystal](../configuring/index.mdx#defining-a-crystal).

## The shared crystal item — `items/crystal.yml`

Each crystal in `content/crystals/` declares its own material/name/lore so it self-explains. The
fields here are the **shared fallback** (used when a crystal omits one) and the look of a **merged**
multi-crystal, whose two effects can't share one material:

```yaml
type: crystal
material: AMETHYST_SHARD
name: "&d{CRYSTAL} Crystal"
lore:
  - "&7Drag onto gear to apply."
  - "&7Merge two crystals into a multi-crystal."
success-chance: 75               # apply success roll
consume-on-fail: true            # a failed apply consumes the crystal
sounds:
  enabled: true
  apply: "block.amethyst_block.chime"
  remove: "block.amethyst_cluster.break"
# The crystal EXTRACTOR — drag onto crystal-bearing gear to pop its last crystal back into an item.
extractor:
  material: AMETHYST_CLUSTER
  name: "&dCrystal Extractor"
  lore:
    - "&7Drag onto crystal-bearing gear"
    - "&7to extract its last crystal."
```

| Token | Renders |
| --- | --- |
| `{CRYSTAL}` | the component crystal name(s) |

## Applying, merging, extracting

- **Apply.** Drag a crystal onto gear; the apply rolls `success-chance`. On a miss, `consume-on-fail`
  decides whether the crystal is lost.
- **Merge.** Drag one crystal onto another to combine them into a **multi-crystal** — two effects in a
  single socket. The merged item's lore is each component's own lore, concatenated.
- **Extract.** The **crystal extractor** pops the last-applied crystal back off socketed gear as a
  whole item.

:::note Sockets are separate from enchant slots
How many crystals a piece can hold is governed by `crystals.slots` (default 1) and the absolute
`crystals.max-stack` (default 16) in [config.yml](../configuring/index.mdx) — a different ledger from
[enchant slots](../concepts/slots.mdx).
:::

## Minting & giving

```text
/se crystal <key>                # mint a specific crystal to yourself (key from content/crystals/)
/se give crystal <player> <key>
/se give extractor <player>      # give a crystal extractor
```
